Transmission medium and protocols in Computer Networks

Transmission medium - It defines the path over which the data get communicated with the sender and receiver.
It is divided into two main categories that is guided and unguided medium .
Guided Medium - Guided medium uses the transmission over the physical medium like copper wires and optical fibers to transmit the data .
The guided medium more secure and reliable data communication and also it has fast speed.
Example of guided medium is when we connect our laptop to hub with the help of cable.
